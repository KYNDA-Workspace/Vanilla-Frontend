How does Kynda connect students with tutors?

Kynda matches students to verified tutors based on subjects, class level, and availability. Students (or parents) can browse tutor profiles, check ratings, and book sessions instantly.

How do I book and pay for a lesson?

You can book a lesson from a tutor's profile and pay securely through our platform using the supported payment methods. Receipts and session details will be available in your dashboard.

What if I need to reschedule or cancel?

You can reschedule or cancel a lesson from your dashboard. Cancellation and rescheduling policies depend on the time before the lesson and the tutor's settings.
